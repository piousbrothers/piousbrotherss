Task 1:
Share Salaam and Make Dua for 10 People
Task:
Begin your day with the beautiful greeting, "السلام عليكم ورحمة الله وبركاته" (Peace, mercy, and blessings of Allah be upon you). Share this greeting with at least 10 people—family, friends, or even strangers. After greeting, take a moment to make a heartfelt dua (prayer) for each of them, asking Allah to bless them, forgive their sins, and grant them goodness in this world and the hereafter.
